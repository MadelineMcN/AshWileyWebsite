Hi. I'm Ash.

Ash Wylie has eight years of experience working in Atlanta’s premier salons before becoming an independent stylist and joining Aviary Beauty + Wellness in 2020. A native Atlantan, Ash got her cosmetology training at the Toni & Guy Hairdressing Academy. After graduating, she went on to assist her first mentor Ben White for several years in a high-volume salon where top notch training was a priority before hitting the floor. Razor cutting is one of Ash’s strengths but she also specializes in effortless cuts and color that will last, grow out and style easily. She’s a master at making bangs work for every face shape, curly hair cutting, and edgy color/cuts. Over her years developing relationships with clients, Ash has a passion for getting to know them, often connecting over life experiences and shared ideals. For Ash, the key to happiness in to be truly present in her relationships. Outside of work, you’ll find her somewhere outside biking or hanging in the park with friends and family. At Aviary, Ash looks forward to her first experience working in a boutique salon setting where she will be able to bring high fashion back down to earth. Book a consultation.
